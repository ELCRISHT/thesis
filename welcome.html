About IntelliGrade

IntelliGrade is an intelligent academic monitoring system designed to assess and analyze AI Dependency among students across multiple colleges, programs, and year levels.

Built using integrated theoretical frameworks such as Cognitive Load Theory (CLT), Self-Determination Theory (SDT), and Social Cognitive Theory (SCT), the system generates personalized insights and recommendations based on students’ usage behavior, motivation, and cognitive patterns.

IntelliGrade helps faculty and administrators make informed decisions through automated analytics, risk detection, report generation, and intuitive dashboard tools.
